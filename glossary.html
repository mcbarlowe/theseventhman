Introduction

This is a glossary that will try to breifly (and maybe sometimes not so briefly) to describe the statistics used by this website. If you see any errors or things that could be better explained please feel free to contact me at matt@barloweanalytics.com


FGM

Field Goals Made: shots made by the player that were not free throws.


FGA

Field Goals Attempted: all shots attempted by the player that were not free throws.


TPM

Three Points Made: all three point shots made by the player.


TPA

Three Points Attempted: all three point shots attempted by the player.


FTM

Free Throws Made: all free throw shots made by the player.


FTA

Free Throws Made: all free throw shots made by the player.


OREB

Offensive Rebounds: offensive rebounds made by the player.


DREB

Defensive Rebounds: offensive rebounds made by the player.


AST

Assists: total assists made by the player.


TOV

Turnovers: total assists made by the player.


PTS

Points: total points made by the player.


STL

Steals: total steals made by the player.


PF

Personal Fouls: total personal fouls commited by the player.

This includes:

  • Shooting Fouls
  • Flagrant Fouls
  • Tehcnical Fouls
  • Reach In Fouls
  • But does not include team fouls

Plus/Minus

Plus Minus: The point differential between when a player is on or off the court.


TOC

Time on Court: Time player spent on the court in seconds.
